I. Arithmetic & Commercial Math

Number Systems & Divisibility

Sum of First n Natural Numbers

\[ \text{Sum} = \frac{n(n + 1)}{2} \]

Sum of First n Odd Numbers

\[ \text{Sum} = n^2 \]

Trailing Zeros (Legendre's Formula)

Exponent of 5 in \(n!\)

\[ E_5(n!) = \sum_{k=1}^{\infty} \lfloor \frac{n}{5^k} \rfloor \]

Divisibility Rules

6: By 2 & 3 | 8: Last 3 digits | 9: Sum of digits | 11: Odd/Even place diff is 0 or \(11k\)

24: By 3 & 8 | 72: By 8 & 9

HCF & LCM

Product Property

\[ n_1 \times n_2 = \text{HCF} \times \text{LCM} \]

HCF & LCM of Fractions

\[ \text{HCF} = \frac{\text{HCF of Num}}{\text{LCM of Den}} \] \[ \text{LCM} = \frac{\text{LCM of Num}}{\text{HCF of Den}} \]

Constant Remainder

If \( (a - r_1) = (b - r_2) = (c - r_3) = k \)

\[ \text{Number} = \text{LCM}(a, b, c) - k \]

Percentage & Commercial

Consumption Adjustment

If A is \(r\%\) more than B, B is less by:

\[ \left[ \frac{r}{100 + r} \right] \times 100\% \]

Decimal Multiplier Rule

\[ \text{Value} = x \cdot (1 \pm \frac{r}{100}) \]

Successive Net Change

\[ a + b + \frac{ab}{100} \]

Profit, Loss & Marked Price

\[ \frac{CP}{MP} = \frac{100 - D\%}{100 + P\%} \]
\[ \text{Profit}\% = \left[ \frac{A - B}{B} \right] \times 100\% \]

(Where CP of A items = SP of B items)

Dishonest Dealer Gain

\[ \text{Gain}\% = \frac{\text{Error}}{\text{True Value} - \text{Error}} \times 100\% \]

Interest & Compounding

Simple Interest (SI)

\[ \text{SI} = \frac{P \times R \times T}{100} \]

SI Doubling Time (Linear Multiplier)

\[ (N - 1) = \frac{R \times T}{100} \]

\(N\) is the multiplication factor.

Compound Interest (CI)

\[ A = P(1 + \frac{R}{100})^n \]
\[ \text{Eff. Rate} = R_1 + R_2 + \frac{R_1 R_2}{100} \]

CI Power Law Rule

If sum turns \(X\) times in \(Y\) years, it scales to \(X^M\) times in \(M \times Y\) years.

CI vs SI Differences

2 Years: \( \text{Diff} = P(\frac{R}{100})^2 \)

3 Years: \( P(\frac{R}{100})^2 \times (3 + \frac{R}{100}) \)

Averages, Work & Movement

Time, Speed & Distance

Late/Early Formula:

\[ \text{Dist} = \frac{S_1 \times S_2}{|S_1 - S_2|} \times \frac{\Delta T}{60} \]

Average Speed (Equal Dist): \( \frac{2xy}{x+y} \)

Boat & Stream Dynamics

\[ S_D = x+y, \quad S_U = x-y \]
\[ x = \frac{D+U}{2}, \quad y = \frac{D-U}{2} \]

Time & Work / Pipes

\[ \frac{1}{T_{total}} = \frac{1}{A} + \frac{1}{B} \]
\[ \frac{n}{h_1} - \frac{\text{Total} - n}{h_2} = \frac{1}{T_{total}} \]

Man-Days Rule

\[ M_1 D_1 H_1 = M_2 D_2 H_2 \]

Inverse Proportion (Seesaw)

\[ x_1 \cdot y_1 = x_2 \cdot y_2 = k \]
