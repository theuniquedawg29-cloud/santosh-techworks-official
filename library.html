Algebra

Quadratic Formula

Standard form for solving any quadratic equation \(ax^2 + bx + c = 0\).

\[x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}\]

Where \(b^2 - 4ac\) is the Discriminant (D).

Statistics

Arithmetic Mean

The average of a set of \(n\) numbers.

\[\bar{x} = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i}{n}\]
Geometry

Pythagoras Theorem

Relation between the sides of a right-angled triangle.

\[a^2 + b^2 = c^2\]

Applicable only to triangles with one 90° angle.

Trigonometry

Trig Identity

The fundamental Pythagorean trigonometric identity.

\[\sin^2\theta + \cos^2\theta = 1\]
Mensuration

Heron's Formula

Area of triangle given three side lengths \(a, b, c\).

\[A = \sqrt{s(s-a)(s-b)(s-c)}\]

\(s\) is the semi-perimeter \((a+b+c)/2\).
