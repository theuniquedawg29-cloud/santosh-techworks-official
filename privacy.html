Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 16, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Quant-Elite. I am Santosh Choubey, the solo developer of this application. I am committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe experience while mastering mathematics. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use my mobile application and website.

By using Quant-Elite, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information I Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Data: When you sign in via Google or Email, I use Firebase Authentication to manage your identity. I collect your name and email address to personalize your experience.
  • User Profile: I store your quiz progress, scores, and certificates to help you track your learning journey.

2.2 Media and Sensors

  • Microphone: With your permission, I access the microphone to enable the "Live Mentor" voice interaction feature. Your voice is converted to text locally or via Google's Speech-to-Text services to interact with the AI.
  • Camera: I access your camera only when you choose to take a photo of a math problem to upload it for AI explanation.
  • Photo Library: When you pick an image from your gallery, I use the system Photo Picker. I do not have broad access to your entire media library.

2.3 Automatically Collected Data

  • Device Info: I collect basic device information (model, OS version) to ensure app stability and debug crashes.
  • Usage Data: I track which topics you study and your quiz performance to provide analytics and improvement suggestions.

3. How I Use Your Information

I use the information to:

  • Provide and maintain the "Ganitacharya AI" mentor service.
  • Sync your progress across devices via Firebase.
  • Generate personalized performance certificates.
  • Show relevant educational advertisements via Google Mobile Ads.
  • Process premium subscriptions via Google Play Billing.
  • Send notifications for practice reminders.

4. Third-Party Services

To provide a feature-rich experience, I use the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, database storage, and crash reporting.
  • Google Gemini AI: To power the "Ganitacharya AI" math mentor. When you send a text or image query, it is processed by Google's AI models.
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob): To display advertisements in the free version of the app.
  • Google Play Services: For core app functionality and in-app purchases.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your chat history with the AI mentor and images you upload are stored locally on your device for your convenience. I also use Firebase (Cloud Firestore) to securely store your scores and profile data so you don't lose them if you change devices. I implement industry-standard security measures, but please remember that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

I do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent and you are aware that your child has provided me with personal data, please contact me so that I can take necessary actions.

7. Your Rights

You can update your profile name within the app at any time. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can do so through the app settings or by contacting me directly at the email provided below.

8. Changes to This Policy

I may update my Privacy Policy from time to time. I will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
